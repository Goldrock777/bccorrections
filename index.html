01Understanding & Commitment

Award us today, and we are fully operational before the November 1 transition.

The Province's basis for a direct award is the risk and cost of onboarding a new supplier within a short bridge term, with specific attention to quality. We take that seriously — and because we manufacture in-house here in BC, we don't carry the long lead times a reseller does. An award now gives roughly four months of runway. Here is exactly how we use it.

WEEKS 1–2
Facility & compliance secured

Secure Canadian warehousing and complete insurance, bonding and compliance paperwork — the administrative groundwork done up front.

14 BUSINESS DAYS
Evaluation samples

Samples of requested items delivered for side-by-side review, built to exceed the quality currently in service. Production begins immediately on approval.

MONTH 1
Ordering platform live

The web-based ordering, points and inventory system configured to the ACD's specification and in service — the system you can use today.

MONTH 2
Officer onboarding & sizing

Officers onboarded to the platform, with on-site fittings and sizing capture across all ten centres to confirm requirements before volume production.

MONTH 3
Full stock — ready for Nov 1

Inventory manufactured in-house and positioned to support all 1,725 officers, with same-day and next-day delivery of in-stock goods across BC.
