How it works

From order to centre, without the paperwork

Every part of the program runs through one platform — ordering, points, fulfilment and oversight.

STEP 01

Sign in

Officers authenticate with their Badge ID and see their live annual points balance.

STEP 02

Configure & order

Choose item, fit, size and quantity from the online catalogue. Points deduct on confirmation.

STEP 03

Fulfil to centre

Orders ship to the officer's assigned centre, tracked from pending to delivered.

STEP 04

Oversee

Administration monitors stock, points usage, delivery performance and compliance in real time.
