How It Works
Order to doorstep, in four steps

The whole program runs through one online system — no paperwork, no phone orders, no guesswork on stock or points.

1

Officer signs in

Each officer logs in with their Badge ID and sees their live annual points balance — 500 points, reset yearly.

2

Orders from the catalogue

Browse 10 standard uniform items, each with a point value. Pick size, colour and quantity — points deduct automatically.

3

Ships to their centre

Every order is delivered to the officer's assigned correctional centre and tracked Pending → Shipped → Delivered.

4

Admin stays in control

Stock across all 10 centres, points usage, delivery performance and compliance are visible live on the admin dashboard.
